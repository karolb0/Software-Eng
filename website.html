About Us



Mission

At Abandon Aviation, our mission is to provide high-quality aviation parts to keep aircraft flying safely while responsibly managing surplus inventory. We connect aviation professionals with reliable parts online and ensure the ethical disposal or repurposing of obsolete components, minimizing waste and maximizing efficiency in the industry.

Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to platform for both acquiring and responsibly decommissioning aviation parts. We strive to lead the industry with innovation, sustainability, and reliability, ensuring that every part finds its best use whether in the air or through environmentally conscious disposal.

Values

At Abandon Aviation, we deliver reliable aviation parts while ensuring responsible disposal, minimizing waste, and prioritizing customer satisfaction with integrity and innovation.

  • Information
